Privacy Policy

App: spnd.  |  Developer: Godi Narayana Swamy  |  Last updated: May 31, 2026  |  Contact: godinarayanaswamy@gmail.com
spnd. does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal or financial data. Everything stays on your device. You can verify this by turning on Airplane Mode — the app works perfectly.

1. Introduction

spnd. is a personal finance tracking app for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how spnd. handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy — spnd. is designed to keep all your financial data private and on your device only.

2. SMS Permission — What We Read and What We Don't

spnd. requests permission to read SMS messages only to detect bank transaction alerts automatically.

What we read:

  • Debit alerts from your bank (e.g. "Rs 500 debited from your account")
  • Credit alerts from your bank (e.g. "Rs 10,000 credited to your account")
  • Messages from known Indian bank senders only (SBI, ICICI, HDFC, Axis, Kotak, and others)

What we NEVER read:

  • OTP (One Time Password) messages — detected and immediately skipped
  • Personal messages from friends or family
  • Promotional or marketing SMS
  • Messages from non-bank senders
SMS permission is optional. Users can skip SMS permission entirely and add transactions manually. The SMS feature can also be turned off anytime in Settings → SMS Import.

3. Data We Collect

  • Bank transaction details extracted from SMS (amount, merchant, date)
  • Transactions you add manually
  • Budget settings and category preferences
  • App settings

4. What We DO NOT Collect

  • Your name, age, or personal identity
  • Contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any advertising or tracking data

5. Data Storage — Everything Stays on Your Device

spnd. has no servers and no cloud storage.
All data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. Nothing is ever uploaded, synced, or transmitted to any server.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with:

  • Any third parties
  • Advertisers or analytics companies
  • Other apps or services
  • Anyone at all

7. Data Security

  • Encrypted database — transactions stored in AES-256 encrypted SQLite
  • No internet transmission — data never leaves your device
  • Optional PIN / biometric lock — lock the app with fingerprint or PIN
  • Screenshot protection — optional screen security to prevent screenshots

9. User Consent

spnd. asks for your explicit permission before accessing SMS messages:

  • During onboarding, the app clearly explains why SMS permission is needed
  • The user must actively grant SMS permission — it is never enabled silently
  • If the user denies SMS permission, the app still works fully with manual entry
  • The user can revoke SMS permission anytime from Android Settings → Apps → spnd. → Permissions
  • The user can also disable SMS reading from within the app: Settings → SMS Import → toggle off

10. Data Retention

Regarding how long we store your data:

  • SMS content — The raw SMS text is stored temporarily only to extract transaction details. Only the transaction data (amount, merchant, date, category) is saved. The original SMS text is stored as a reference only and never transmitted anywhere.
  • Transaction data — Stored on your device for as long as you use the app. Deleted immediately when you delete a transaction or uninstall the app.
  • Settings and preferences — Stored locally and deleted on uninstall.
  • We do not have any servers, so there is no data stored outside your device at any time.

11. Children's Privacy

spnd. is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
